
1. Prompt: "Provide a step-by-step recipe for Kung Pao Chicken."
   Result:
   - Ingredients: 500g chicken breast (diced), 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp cornstarch, 3 tbsp cooking oil, 10 dried red chilies, 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1/2 cup roasted peanuts, 1/4 cup diced green onions.
   - Sauce: 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp hoisin sauce, 1/2 cup water.
   - Instructions:
     1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch for 15 minutes.
     2. Heat oil in a wok, stir-fry dried chilies until aromatic.
     3. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove from wok.
     4. Stir-fry garlic and green onions. Add sauce ingredients and cook until thickened.
     5. Return chicken to the wok, mix well with sauce, and toss in peanuts. Serve hot.

2. Prompt: "How can I make authentic Mapo Tofu at home?"
   Result:
   - Ingredients: 1 block of firm tofu (cubed), 200g ground pork, 2 tbsp doubanjiang, 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns, 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 tbsp ginger (minced), 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1/2 cup chicken stock, 1 tsp cornstarch, green onions for garnish.
   - Instructions:
     1. Heat oil in a wok, add Sichuan peppercorns, and fry until fragrant.
     2. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, and doubanjiang for 1 minute.
     3. Add ground pork and cook until browned.
     4. Pour in chicken stock and soy sauce, then add tofu.
     5. Simmer for 5 minutes, thicken with cornstarch, and garnish with green onions.

3. Prompt: "Describe how to prepare homemade Chinese Dumplings (Jiaozi)."
   Result:
   - Ingredients: 300g ground pork, 1 cup minced cabbage, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 pack of dumpling wrappers, water for sealing.
   - Instructions:
     1. Mix pork, cabbage, soy sauce, and sesame oil in a bowl.
     2. Place 1 tsp filling on a wrapper, wet the edges, fold, and seal tightly.
     3. Boil in water for 5 minutes or steam for 10 minutes. Serve with dipping sauce.

4. Prompt: "What is the recipe for Hot and Sour Soup?"
   Result:
   - Ingredients: 4 cups chicken broth, 100g tofu (sliced), 50g mushrooms, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tsp white pepper, 1 beaten egg.
   - Instructions:
     1. Bring chicken broth to a boil, add tofu and mushrooms.
     2. Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, and white pepper.
     3. Slowly pour the beaten egg into the soup while stirring.
     4. Simmer for 2 minutes and serve.
